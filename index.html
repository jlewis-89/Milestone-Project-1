Meet the Directors

Mark

Mark has built a career specialising in development, with a strong emphasis on large mixed-use urban and suburban regeneration projects. He has full development lifecycle expertise covering project identification and acquisition, master planning, construction, leasing, funding and investment sale. He leads on community benefit projects both locally and nationally. Glen’s strengths lie in managing risk, programming, planning construction procurement, Contract Negotiation, full management of the construction process.
